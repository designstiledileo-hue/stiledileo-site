What is Venetian Plaster?

Venetian plaster is a natural mineral-based finish made from lime, marble dust, and pigments. It is applied in multiple layers and polished to create a smooth or textured surface that mimics natural stone.

This technique has been used for centuries in European architecture and is now widely used in luxury homes across Vancouver.

Cost of Venetian Plaster in Vancouver

The cost of Venetian plaster in Vancouver depends on the finish, complexity, and size of the project. On average, premium Venetian plaster ranges between $18–$35 per square foot.

Custom marble effects, high-gloss finishes, and detailed work can increase the price due to additional layers and craftsmanship.

Where Venetian Plaster Works Best

Venetian plaster can be used in a variety of spaces:

  • Living room feature walls
  • Fireplace surrounds
  • Bedrooms
  • Dining areas
  • Luxury bathrooms with proper sealing

In Vancouver homes, it is especially popular in modern condos, high-end houses, and custom-built interiors.

Is Venetian Plaster Better Than Paint?

While paint is cheaper, Venetian plaster offers a completely different level of design. It is not just a finish — it is a statement.

Unlike paint, Venetian plaster:

  • Has texture and depth
  • Reflects light beautifully
  • Does not look flat
  • Lasts for decades
