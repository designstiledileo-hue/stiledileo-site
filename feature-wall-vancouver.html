Feature Wall Cost in Vancouver

Most projects range between $2,000 – $5,000+ depending on wall size, preparation, finish type, and design complexity.

Simpler textured walls cost less, while custom marble effects, multi-layer finishes, and premium detailing increase the overall investment.

Custom Design Approach

Every feature wall is designed individually. We consider lighting, proportions, surrounding materials, and the overall mood of the room to ensure a cohesive and high-end result.

The goal is not simply to decorate a wall, but to create a surface that feels integrated into the architecture of the home.

Feature Walls vs Paint

A painted wall can add colour, but a Venetian plaster feature wall adds depth, movement, light reflection, and a custom feel that paint alone cannot deliver.

This is why feature walls are often chosen in higher-end Vancouver homes, condos, and renovated interiors where visual impact matters.

Perfect for Modern Vancouver Interiors

Vancouver interiors often favour clean lines, soft neutral palettes, and elevated materials. Venetian plaster feature walls work beautifully in this context because they create richness without visual clutter.

They pair especially well with fireplaces, custom millwork, oak finishes, stone details, and contemporary lighting.
