Feature Walls
Vancouver

A feature wall can completely transform a space. At Stile di Leo, we design and install custom feature walls in Vancouver using Venetian plaster and premium decorative finishes.

Each project is handcrafted to create depth, movement, and a refined luxury aesthetic that cannot be achieved with paint.

Popular Feature Wall Styles

  • Soft textured neutral finishes
  • Minimalist luxury surfaces
  • Warm tones with natural movement

Where Feature Walls Work Best

  • Living rooms
  • Bedrooms
  • Dining areas
  • Condos and modern apartments
  • Open-concept interiors

Feature Wall Cost in Vancouver

Most feature walls range between $2,000 – $5,000+ depending on size, surface condition, and design complexity.

Custom Design Approach

Every wall is designed individually. We consider lighting, proportions, and surrounding materials to ensure the finish integrates perfectly into the space.
