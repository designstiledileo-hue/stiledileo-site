Is Venetian Plaster Suitable for Bathrooms?

Yes — when applied correctly and sealed properly, Venetian plaster can be used in bathrooms. It provides a smooth, continuous finish without grout lines, which makes the space feel more modern and visually clean.

It is especially effective in powder rooms, feature walls, and areas outside direct water exposure.

Microcement vs Venetian Plaster

For full wet areas like showers, microcement is often used as an alternative. However, Venetian plaster remains a preferred choice for bathroom feature walls due to its softness, depth, and natural look.

Where It Works Best

Venetian plaster is ideal for:

  • Bathroom feature walls

Why Choose This Finish

Compared to tile, Venetian plaster offers a more refined and architectural look. It works especially well in high-end homes where design continuity and material quality are important.

The result feels softer, more elevated, and more integrated with the rest of the interior than a standard tiled wall.
