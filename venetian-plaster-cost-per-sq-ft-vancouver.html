Typical Venetian Plaster Cost Per Sq Ft in Vancouver

  • $18 to $25 per sq ft for simpler finishes on straightforward prepared walls
  • $25 to $35 per sq ft for richer movement, premium textures, and stronger focal surfaces
  • $35+ per sq ft for marble-look work, niche detailing, curves, and higher-complexity applications

These are planning ranges, not fixed quote promises. Final cost depends on the actual room, substrate, and finish direction.

What Changes the Per-Square-Foot Rate

  • Wall preparation and substrate condition
  • Finish complexity and amount of movement
  • Wall height, access, and project logistics
  • Whether the wall includes fireplace detailing, corners, niches, or transitions
  • Whether the finish is decorative, marble-look, or more restrained and minimal

Feature Walls vs Fireplace Walls

A simple rectangular feature wall is usually more predictable on a square-foot basis than a fireplace composition. Fireplace walls often involve additional layout complexity, cut lines, transitions, and more visible finish expectations.

For those narrower use cases, review Feature Wall Vancouver and Fireplace Wall Vancouver.

When Sq Ft Pricing Is Useful and When It Is Not

Square-foot pricing is useful early in planning, but it becomes less precise on smaller walls, custom fireplace details, and premium statement surfaces where visual quality matters more than raw area alone. In many projects, the finish direction influences cost as much as the wall size.
