Home Staging vs Interior Design: What Vancouver Sellers Actually Need

Home staging and interior design are often confused, but they serve different goals. If you are preparing to sell a property in Vancouver, the difference matters.

What Home Staging Does

Staging is designed to increase buyer appeal. It focuses on layout, perceived space, emotional response, and market presentation.

What Interior Design Does

Interior design is created for long-term living. It responds to lifestyle, personal taste, comfort, and deeper material choices.

What Sellers Usually Need

Most sellers need staging, not full design. But premium properties may benefit from selective design upgrades before staging.

Where Decorative Finishes Fit In

Luxury wall finishes, Venetian plaster details, and carefully chosen feature moments can elevate value when applied strategically.
