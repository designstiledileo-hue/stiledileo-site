What is a Feature Wall?

A feature wall (also called an accent wall) is a single wall in a room designed to stand out. It can be created using texture, color, material, or lighting to draw attention and define the space.

In Vancouver homes, feature walls are commonly used in living rooms, bedrooms, and around fireplaces.

Why Feature Walls Are Popular in Vancouver

Modern interiors in Vancouver often follow a minimalist approach. A feature wall adds character without cluttering the space.

  • Creates a focal point in open layouts
  • Adds texture and depth
  • Enhances lighting and reflections
  • Increases perceived property value
