How It Compares to Paint

Paint can make a wall look clean, but it rarely creates the same depth and light movement as Venetian plaster. For Burnaby clients who want a more finished and custom result, plaster often feels more appropriate in modern interiors.

It becomes part of the architecture instead of just a coating on top of drywall.

Burnaby Design Direction: Clean, Refined, Urban

Many Burnaby interiors benefit from finishes that feel calm and sophisticated rather than overly decorative. Venetian plaster supports that direction well because it introduces richness through material quality, not visual noise.

For homeowners who want something more premium than paint but more flexible than full slab installation, it is often the right middle ground.
