Designed for Burnaby Condos, Townhomes, and Refined Family Homes

Burnaby has a distinct mix of high-rise living and spacious townhouse communities. That means finishes need to look elevated up close, perform well under natural window light, and complement cabinetry, stone, and modern flooring without overpowering the room.

Venetian plaster is especially effective in condo interiors because it adds depth without bulky paneling or heavy slabs. It gives compact living spaces a more intentional, upscale atmosphere.

Why Burnaby Clients Choose Venetian Plaster Over Paint

Paint can make a wall look neat, but it does not interact with light in the same way. Venetian plaster has tonal variation, subtle movement, and a material quality that makes the finish feel integrated into the interior rather than applied on top of it.

In newer Burnaby homes with large windows, custom millwork, and modern fireplaces, that difference is immediately visible.
