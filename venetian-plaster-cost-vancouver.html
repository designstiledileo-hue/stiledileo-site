Average Venetian Plaster Cost Per Square Foot in Vancouver

  • $18 to $25 per sq ft for cleaner, simpler finishes on straightforward walls
  • $25 to $35 per sq ft for premium textures, refined movement, and higher-detail surfaces
  • $35+ per sq ft for marble-look work, layered finishes, curves, niches, and more complex applications

Small custom projects can price higher on a per-square-foot basis because setup, protection, and detailing are still required even when the wall itself is modest in size.

What Affects Venetian Plaster Pricing Most

Several variables can shift a project into a higher or lower range:

  • Existing wall condition, patching, skim work, and substrate preparation
  • Desired finish, from soft mineral texture to polished marble-look veining
  • Wall height, corners, returns, niches, and integration around fireplaces or millwork
  • Site access, parking, elevator logistics, and protection requirements
  • How visible the wall is under daylight, pot lights, and side lighting

A flat matte feature wall and a high-contrast marble fireplace wall require very different levels of craftsmanship, which is why pricing can vary significantly even at similar sizes.

What Is Usually Included in a Quote

Most Venetian plaster quotes cover surface review, minor preparation, masking and protection, material supply, handcrafted application, and finishing work appropriate to the selected effect.

If a wall needs major drywall correction, demolition, extensive substrate replacement, or unusual site coordination, those items are usually scoped separately so the final quote remains accurate.

Fireplace Wall Pricing in Vancouver

Fireplace walls often cost more than simple flat walls because they involve reveals, returns, edges, cutouts, and a higher level of visual scrutiny. Many clients also choose richer finishes for fireplace focal points.

  • Simple fireplace surround: $3,000 to $4,000
  • TV and fireplace composition: $4,000 to $5,500
  • Marble-look fireplace wall: $5,500 and up

Why Vancouver Quotes Can Vary

Vancouver projects range from downtown condos with loading and elevator restrictions to larger West Side and North Shore homes with taller walls and more exacting finish standards. Labour planning, protection, access, and staging all affect pricing before the plaster itself is applied.

That is why online numbers are useful for budgeting, but an actual quote is still the most accurate way to compare options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Venetian plaster cost per square foot in Vancouver?
Most projects fall between $18 and $35+ per square foot, depending on the finish, preparation, and complexity.

Why does a small wall sometimes cost more per square foot?
Smaller projects still require setup, masking, material preparation, and finishing time, so the unit rate can be higher than on larger walls.

What is included in the quoted price?
Quotes usually include preparation, protection, materials, application, and finishing. Major drywall repair or unusual site conditions may be priced separately.

Can I get a budget range from photos?
Yes. Photos, approximate measurements, and a short description of the finish you want are usually enough for a preliminary pricing range.
