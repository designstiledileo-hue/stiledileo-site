Venetian Plaster Cost

A 2026 pricing guide for Venetian plaster feature walls, fireplace walls, and premium decorative finishes across Vancouver and the Lower Mainland.

Venetian plaster is one of the most premium wall finishes available in Vancouver. It offers a natural, stone-like appearance and a level of craftsmanship that standard paint simply cannot achieve.

If you’re considering Venetian plaster for your home, one of the first questions is cost. In this guide, we break down pricing, the factors that affect it, and what to expect when investing in a luxury wall finish in Vancouver.

Average Cost of Venetian Plaster in Vancouver

The cost of Venetian plaster in Vancouver typically ranges between:

  • $18 – $25 per sq ft for standard finishes
  • $25 – $35 per sq ft for custom or premium finishes
  • $35+ per sq ft for high-end marble effects and detailed work

Each project is unique, and final pricing depends on multiple factors, including design complexity, surface condition, and the level of finish required.

What Affects the Cost?

Several factors influence the price of Venetian plaster in Vancouver:

  • Surface preparation such as drywall, tile, or uneven walls
  • Number of layers required
  • Finish type such as matte, polished, or marble effect
  • Level of detail and custom design work
  • Overall project size

For example, a simple feature wall will cost significantly less than a large fireplace wall with custom veining, polishing, and more intensive preparation.

Cost for Different Projects

Here is a general breakdown for common projects in Vancouver:

  • Small feature wall: $1,500 – $3,000
  • Fireplace wall: $2,500 – $6,000+
  • Full accent wall: varies based on size and design

Is Venetian Plaster Worth the Price?

While Venetian plaster costs more than paint, it delivers significantly more value in terms of design, durability, and long-term visual impact.

Unlike paint, Venetian plaster:

  • Creates depth and movement
  • Reflects light in a natural way
  • Is highly durable
  • Adds a luxury feel to the space

For homeowners in Vancouver who want to elevate their interiors, it is often worth the investment.

How to Get an Accurate Quote

The best way to understand pricing is to evaluate your specific space. Every project is different, and a proper quote depends on measurements, wall condition, and design preferences.
