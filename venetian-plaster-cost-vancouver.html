Venetian Plaster Cost
Vancouver

How much does Venetian plaster cost in Vancouver? This page focuses on real pricing ranges, cost per square foot, project examples, and the factors that change the final quote.

Venetian plaster pricing in Vancouver depends on more than just wall size. Surface preparation, finish type, project access, and the amount of detailing all affect the final number.

If you are comparing options for a feature wall, fireplace wall, or a marble-effect decorative surface, this guide gives a practical overview of what to expect before requesting a quote.

Average Venetian Plaster Cost Per Square Foot in Vancouver

  • $18 – $25 per sq ft for simpler finishes and more straightforward walls
  • $25 – $35 per sq ft for premium finishes and more custom surfaces
  • $35+ per sq ft for detailed marble effects, layered luxury finishes, and more complex work

These are general Vancouver pricing ranges. The actual quote depends on wall condition, desired effect, and the amount of labour involved.

Feature Wall Pricing in Vancouver

A feature wall is usually the most common Venetian plaster project. Final pricing depends on dimensions, condition, and how custom the finish needs to be.

  • Small feature wall: $1,500 – $2,500
  • Medium statement wall: $2,500 – $4,000
  • Larger custom wall: $4,000+

If the wall requires more repair, strong lighting reveals imperfections, or the design includes marble-style veining, the price increases.

Fireplace Wall Pricing in Vancouver

Fireplace walls are often more expensive than simple flat walls because they usually involve edges, returns, cut lines, transitions, and more visible detailing.

  • Simple fireplace surround: $2,500 – $3,500
  • Custom fireplace wall: $3,500 – $5,000
  • Marble-effect fireplace wall: $5,000 – $6,000+

What Increases the Cost?

Several factors can move a project into a higher price range:

  • Uneven drywall, damaged surfaces, or tile that needs preparation
  • Custom marble veining and higher-detail artistic work
  • Multiple layers and polishing
  • Large walls or difficult access conditions
  • Premium finish expectations under strong natural or artificial lighting

A simple matte wall and a designer marble-effect wall are not priced the same, even when the square footage is similar.

Why Pricing Can Differ Across Vancouver Projects

Vancouver projects often vary because the properties themselves vary. A downtown condo may require elevator logistics, stricter protection, and tighter working conditions, while a larger home in West Vancouver or North Vancouver may involve higher walls, larger surfaces, and more premium finish expectations.

This is why cost pages should be used as a guide, not as a fixed quote.

Is Venetian Plaster More Expensive Than Paint?

Yes. Venetian plaster is more expensive than paint because it is a handcrafted finish, not a standard coating. It takes more preparation, more labour, and more finish control.

But it also delivers a very different result: more depth, more movement, and a much more custom look.

How to Get an Accurate Price

The best way to price Venetian plaster is by reviewing:

  • wall dimensions
  • surface condition
  • desired finish style
  • photos of the space

A quick online range is useful, but a real quote always depends on the actual wall and finish direction.
