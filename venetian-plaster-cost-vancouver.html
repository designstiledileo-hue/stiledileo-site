Venetian Plaster Cost
Vancouver

A 2026 pricing guide for Venetian plaster feature walls, fireplace walls, marble-effect finishes, and premium decorative surfaces across Vancouver and the Lower Mainland.

Venetian plaster is one of the most premium wall finishes available in Vancouver. It offers a natural, stone-like appearance and a level of craftsmanship that standard paint simply cannot achieve.

If you’re considering Venetian plaster for your home, one of the first questions is cost. This guide breaks down realistic price ranges, the factors that influence cost, and what to expect when investing in a custom wall finish in Vancouver.

Average Cost of Venetian Plaster in Vancouver

The cost of Venetian plaster in Vancouver typically ranges between:

  • $18 – $25 per sq ft for standard finishes
  • $25 – $35 per sq ft for custom or premium finishes
  • $35+ per sq ft for detailed marble effects and high-end designer surfaces

Final pricing depends on the type of wall, the level of preparation required, and how refined or custom the finish needs to be.

What Affects the Cost?

Several factors influence the price of Venetian plaster in Vancouver:

  • Surface preparation such as drywall repair, tile coverage, or uneven walls
  • Number of layers required
  • Finish type such as matte, polished, stone-inspired, or marble effect
  • Level of detail and custom design work
  • Overall project size and access conditions

For example, a simple feature wall will cost significantly less than a large fireplace wall with custom veining, polished depth, and more intensive preparation.

Cost for Different Projects

Here is a general pricing breakdown for common projects in Vancouver:

  • Small feature wall: $1,500 – $3,000
  • Medium statement wall: $2,500 – $4,500
  • Fireplace wall: $2,500 – $6,000+
  • Large custom marble-look wall: varies depending on detailing and scale

Why Pricing Varies Across Vancouver Homes

Venetian plaster pricing in Vancouver often varies based on the type of property and the finish expectations. Downtown condos may require tighter protection, elevator logistics, and cleaner installation planning, while larger homes in West Vancouver or North Vancouver often involve bigger surfaces, taller walls, and more premium marble-effect detailing.

This is why two walls of similar size can still have different price ranges depending on preparation, access, design goals, and finish complexity.

Is Venetian Plaster Worth the Price?

While Venetian plaster costs more than paint, it delivers significantly more value in terms of design, durability, and long-term visual impact.

In areas like West Vancouver and North Vancouver, clients often choose premium marble-effect finishes for fireplace walls and feature spaces because the result feels far more architectural and custom than a flat painted surface.

Unlike paint, Venetian plaster:

  • Creates depth and movement
  • Reflects light in a more natural way
  • Offers a handcrafted, premium look
  • Can remain visually relevant for many years

For homeowners in Vancouver who want to elevate their interiors, it is often worth the investment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Venetian plaster feature wall cost in Vancouver?
Most feature walls start around $1,500 and can exceed $3,000 depending on size, preparation, and finish type.

Is Venetian plaster more expensive than paint?
Yes, but it delivers a far more premium result, greater depth, and stronger long-term visual value.

Are fireplace walls more expensive?
Usually yes, because fireplace walls often require more detailing, edge work, and a more architectural finish.

Does the finish type affect price?
Absolutely. A simple matte finish costs less than a custom marble-effect wall with multiple layers, veining, and polishing.
