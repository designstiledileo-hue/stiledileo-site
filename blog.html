Feature Walls Vancouver

A feature wall can completely transform a space. At Stile di Leo, we design and create custom feature walls in Vancouver using Venetian plaster and luxury decorative finishes.

Each surface is handcrafted to introduce depth, movement, and a refined architectural feel that standard paint cannot achieve.

Popular Feature Wall Styles

  • Marble-effect Venetian plaster
  • Soft textured neutral walls
  • Modern minimalist finishes
  • Warm luxury tones with subtle movement

Where Feature Walls Work Best

  • Living rooms
  • Bedrooms
  • Dining areas
  • Modern condos
  • Open-concept interiors

Feature Wall Cost in Vancouver

Most projects range between $2,000 – $5,000+ depending on size, preparation, and design complexity.

Custom Design Approach

Every feature wall is designed individually. We consider lighting, proportions, and surrounding materials to ensure a cohesive and high-end result.
