Custom Feature Walls in Surrey Homes

Surrey homes often benefit from a feature wall that adds depth and material richness without making the room feel heavy. Venetian plaster works especially well for this because it creates movement, softness, and a more architectural finish than standard painted drywall.

These walls can be designed as subtle neutral backdrops or as bold statement surfaces depending on the style of the home.
