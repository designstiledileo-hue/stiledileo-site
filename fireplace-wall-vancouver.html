Modern fireplace wall ideas using Venetian plaster, marble effects, and custom luxury finishes.

A fireplace wall is often the centerpiece of a living room. In Vancouver homes, more homeowners are moving away from basic drywall and choosing high-end finishes that create a stronger architectural presence.

At Stile di Leo, we design custom fireplace walls using Venetian plaster and decorative finishes tailored to modern interiors.

Venetian Plaster Fireplace Walls

Venetian plaster is one of the most popular choices for fireplace walls in Vancouver. It creates a seamless, stone-like finish that feels both modern and timeless.

  • Soft matte or polished finishes
  • Custom marble effects
  • Seamless luxury look

Marble Look Fireplace Walls

Real marble can be expensive and complex to install. Venetian plaster allows you to achieve a similar visual effect while maintaining flexibility in design and cost.

Dark Feature Fireplace Walls

Black and charcoal finishes are increasingly popular in Vancouver luxury homes. These tones create contrast and highlight the fireplace flame as the focal point.

Minimal Modern Design

Clean lines, seamless surfaces, and soft textures are ideal for modern condos and new builds.

How to Choose the Right Style

  • Room size and proportions
  • Natural and artificial lighting
  • Interior style and materials
  • Desired atmosphere (warm, bold, minimal)
