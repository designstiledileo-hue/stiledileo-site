Fireplace Wall Ideas Vancouver

A fireplace wall is often the centerpiece of a living room. In Vancouver homes, homeowners are moving away from basic drywall and exploring high-end finishes.

1. Venetian Plaster Fireplace Walls

This is one of the most popular choices in Vancouver. Venetian plaster creates a seamless, stone-like look that feels both modern and timeless.

  • Soft matte or polished finishes
  • Custom marble effects
  • Works with minimal or luxury interiors

2. Marble Look Walls

Real marble is expensive and difficult to install. Venetian plaster offers a similar look at a more practical cost.

3. Dark Feature Walls

Black or charcoal plaster walls are becoming popular in Vancouver luxury homes. They create strong contrast and highlight the fire.

4. Minimal Modern Design

Clean lines, no visible seams, and a smooth finish — perfect for modern condos and new builds.

How to Choose the Right Style

Consider:

  • Room size
  • Lighting
  • Furniture style
  • Desired mood (warm, bold, minimal)
