Fireplace Wall Design in Vancouver

A fireplace is the focal point of any living space. At Stile di Leo, we create custom fireplace walls in Vancouver using Venetian plaster and decorative finishes designed to elevate modern interiors.

Instead of traditional tile or stone, our finishes offer a seamless, sculptural look that feels both minimal and luxurious.

Modern Fireplace Wall Finishes

Why Clients Choose Custom Fireplace Walls

Custom plaster finishes create a clean, continuous surface with no visible seams or joints. This gives your fireplace a high-end architectural presence.

  • No grout lines
  • Fully custom design
  • Heat-resistant finishes
  • More affordable than real marble slabs
