About

Rooted in Craft, Built on Artistry

Stile Di Leo specializes in Venetian plaster, feature walls, fireplace walls, and refined decorative finishes for Vancouver, West Vancouver, and Yaletown homes. Every project is approached with restraint, material sensitivity, and a premium design eye.

We create interiors that feel composed, expensive, and deeply considered — from statement fireplaces to subtle mineral textures that quietly elevate an entire room.

Our work is shaped by European design language, natural materials, and a belief that luxury is not clutter. It is precision, balance, and atmosphere.
