Top 5 Luxury Home Decor Trends Defining Vancouver Homes in 2026

Luxury interiors in Vancouver are becoming more refined, tactile, and emotionally intentional. In 2026, premium homes are moving away from excess and toward atmosphere, materiality, and design restraint.

1. Sculptural Minimalism

Luxury is no longer about filling a room. It is about choosing fewer, stronger pieces with architectural presence and clean composition.

2. Biophilic Design

Natural textures, stone, wood, moss, soft linen, and filtered light are shaping more restorative interiors across Vancouver and West Vancouver.

3. Venetian Plaster Feature Walls

Venetian plaster continues to stand out as one of the most elegant alternatives to paint, tile, and slab cladding for premium interiors.

4. Rich, Warm Palettes

Warm neutrals, deep olive, charcoal, muted bronze, and layered earth tones are defining today’s luxury spaces.
